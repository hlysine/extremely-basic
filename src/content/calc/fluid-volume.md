Calculate daily fluid requirement with maintenance, deficit and ongoing loss

- Fluid maintenance

  | Weight   | Rate          |
  |----------|---------------|
  | 0-10 kg  | 100 ml/kg/day |
  | 10-20 kg | 50 ml/kg/day  |
  | >20 kg   | 20 ml/kg/day  |

- Replace deficit
  - `kg weight * % deficit * 10` in ml

  | % deficit | Sensorium | Mucus membrane | Skin turgor  | Eyes   | BP     | Pulse  | Urine     |
  |-----------|-----------|----------------|--------------|--------|--------|--------|-----------|
  | 3-5%      | Normal    | Dry            | Normal       | Normal | Normal | Normal | Normal    |
  | 6-10%     | Lethargic | Dry            | Decreased    | Sunken | Normal | High   | Decreased |
  | 10-15%    | Semicoma  | Parched        | Decreased    | Sunken | Low    | High   | Decreased |
  | >15%      | Coma      | Parched        | Cold and dry | Sunken | Low    | Weak   | Absent    |
