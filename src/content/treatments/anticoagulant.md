# Anticoagulation

- **Indication** - acute coronary syndrome

---------------

## Unfractionated heparin (UFH)

- **Administration**
  - **IV bolus** - 60 U/kg (max 4000 U)

## Low molecular weight heparin (LMWH)

- **Administration**
  - **Enoxaparin** - 30 mg IV and 1 mg/kg SC
