# Corticosteroids

- **Indication** - Reduce inflammation in severe COPD / asthma exacerbation
- Taper dose over 2 weeks

------------------

## Hydrocortisone

- **Administration**
  - **IV** - 100-200 mcg 6 hourly

## Methylprednisolone

- **Administration**
  - **IV** - 60-125 mg 6 hourly

## Prednisolone

- **Administration**
  - **Oral** - 30-40 mg daily
