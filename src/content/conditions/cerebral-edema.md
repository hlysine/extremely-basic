# Cerebral edema

## Features

- Gradual coma or sudden deterioration with loss of consciousness
- Fixed dilated pupils
- Respiratory arrest
- Usually 4-12 hours after treatment of DKA or hypernatremia

## Management

- Exclude hypoglycemia
- 0.5 g/kg IV mannitol over 5-10 mins
- Monitor ICP
- Hyperventilation
- CT brain
