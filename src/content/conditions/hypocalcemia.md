# Hypocalcemia

## Causes

- Rule out pseudo-hypocalcemia from hypoalbuminemia
  - Ionized Ca or corrected Ca
- Rule out hypomagnesemia
- Genuine hypocalcemia
  - Chronic renal failure
  - Hypoparathyroidism
  - PTH resistance
- Sequestration of calcium
  - Acute pancreatitis
  - Rhabdomyolysis
  - Tumor lysis syndrome
- Vitamin D deficiency
- Drug-induced
  - Bisphosphonate
  - Cinacalcet

## Investigations

- Serum ionized calcium
- Renal function test
- Albumin
- Phosphate
- ALP
- Mg
- Parathyroid hormone
- Vitamin D level
- Amylase
- CK
- ECG
  - Long QT

## Management

### Symptomatic hypocalcemia

- Correct hypomagnesemia
- Calcium gluconate 10% IV 10-20 ml in 100 ml NS over 15 minutes
  - Then Calcium gluconate 10% IV 20-30 ml in 500 ml NS q6h
- Monitor calcium level

### Chronic hypocalcemia

- Calcium supplement
  - Caltrate
    - 1 tablet = 600 mg elemental Ca
  - Oscal
    - 1 tablet = 250 mg elemental Ca
  - Calcium gluconate
    - 1 tablet = 27 mg elemental Ca
- Vitamin D
  - Calcitriol 0.25-2 mcg daily
- Correct hypomagnesemia
