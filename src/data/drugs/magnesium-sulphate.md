# Magnesium sulphate

- **Indication** - Severe asthma exacerbation not responding to bronchodilators
- **Type** - Calcium channel blocker, bronchodilator
- **Administration**
  - **IV** - single dose of 1.2-2 g or 10-15 mmol over 20 minutes
