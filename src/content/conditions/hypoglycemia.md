# Hypoglycemia

## Causes

- Drugs
  - Insulin
  - Oral hypoglycemics
- Severe critical illness
- Insulinoma
- Liver failure with alcoholism

## Management

- IV thiamine before glucose
- IV 50 ml 50% glucose bolus
  - Then continuous 10% glucose infusion
