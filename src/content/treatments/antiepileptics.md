# Anti-epileptics

## Lorazepam

> First line, require IV access

- **Administration**
  - **IV** - 0.1 mg/kg at 2 mg/min IV

## Midazolam

> Alternative if no IV access

- **Administration**
  - **IM** - 10 mg IM

## Diazepam + Phenytoin

> Second line, require IV access

- **Administration**
  - **IV** - Diazepam 0.2 mg/kg at 5 mg/min IV
    - Loading dose
  - **IV** - Phenytoin 15-20 mg/kg at <50 mg/min IV
    - Maintenance dose
